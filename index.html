What are miniPops?

They are little bite-sized fluffy sweet honey spheres, originally called Loukumades, which are deep fried to golden and crispy perfection. They are served soaked in hot honey syrup, sprinkled with cinnamon or toppings of your choice. The dough of our Puffs is yeast based and made with simple ingredients such as flour, cinnamon and water.
